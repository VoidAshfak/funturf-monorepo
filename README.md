## 🎯 Why FunTurf?

We play a lot of matches on turf grounds — football above all. Friends from school, college, the office fix a date and arrange a match. Booking the ground is easy. **Finding the players is not.**

- You booked the turf but you're three players short.
- So you spam Facebook groups and message everyone in your contacts: *"anyone free tonight?"*
- Or the opposite — *you* want to play today, but you don't have a team.

**FunTurf fixes this.** A player looking for a game and a squad short one striker find each other in seconds. Both sides get what they need — no group-chat chaos, no last-minute cancellations.

## ✨ What You Can Do

### 🙋 Looking to play?
- **Discover matches** happening near you — filter by sport, date, and location.
- **Join any game** that needs players. Request a spot, or get invited by an organizer.
- **Accept or reject invitations** on your terms — no more being added to games you never agreed to.
- **Chat in real time** with everyone in your match, and **message any player** directly.
- Build a **player profile** that shows the sports you play, games you've organized, and the teammates you've connected with.

### 📅 Organizing a match?
- **Create a game** in minutes — set how many players you need and let FunTurf fill the rest.
- **Manage your roster live** — see who's in, who's pending, approve or decline requests.
- **Rematch in one tap** — bring the same squad back together; invitations go out automatically.
- **Book the ground** and attach it to your match, so everyone knows where and when.
- **QR check-in** at the turf — quick, clean, no confusion at the gate.

### 🏟️ Booking a ground?
- **Browse turfs** with photos, facilities, location on the map, and player ratings.
- **See real prices** up front — peak hours and weekends are calculated automatically, no surprises.
- **Apply coupons** at checkout and watch the discount land on your bill instantly.
- **Rate the turfs** you play on and read honest ratings from other players before you book.

### 🏢 Running a turf?
- **List your grounds** through a simple step-by-step setup.
- **Create coupons & promotions** — percentage or flat discounts, for everyone or specific players, valid on the days and dates you choose.
- **Track your business** with clear charts — revenue, bookings, and how your promotions are performing.
